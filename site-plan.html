Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide booking and information about rooms at a hotel chain located near The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints temple locations. It provides not only the rooms and accomodations themselves but also ammenties designed to make it easier for patrons to attend the temple sessions.

Audience

The audience should primarily be adult temple-goers looking to travel and stay near the temple with or without their families. These clients are people who are willing to adhere to the terms and policies of the website. People attending sealings, weddings, and other ordinances are to be taken into consideration.
