Stay A While

Don't worry about getting snowed in! Here in Soda Springs, we have you covered. Even if you have to stay a week or two longer than planned because a blizzard came through and knocked out the roads, there's nothing to worry about. We have all the free WiFi you could ever hope for, and more important than that, we know how to keep warm! You may find you came here on spring break, then a freak snowstorm rolled through. We know how to handle this, isn't the first time it's happened! With top of the line and well loved ski lofts, there is no lack of places to stay, and we'll keep you secure until the snow melts enough for you to escape to literally greener pastures.

Here's a quick story about a family this happened to. The Salvado's were here just last year during the start of March, and they were less prepared for the snow than theye should have been! But it was alright, when they got stuck here a whole extra month, they had the time of their lives. Even their three-year-old daughter Hannah got so good at skiing while here in Soda Springs that she came back the next year and made it down a black diamond trail with no problems. They had hot chocolate every day and nothing to complain about even if they couldn't go home. Sometimes it seemed like they would never leave, and boy they sure were happy about that! So come and hang out with us, go to one of our upcoming events--we have plenty! And if you like soda, we'd love to talk about it with you. We aren't named Soda Springs for nothing!
