Aloha Mililani

As the “Voice of Business” in Mililani, the Mililani Chamber of Commerce is a non-profit organization whose mission is to serve as the advocate for business in Mililani.

The Chamber works on behalf of its 200+ member organizations to advance the city’s economic climate and help the business community to thrive locally, nationally and globally.

  • Connects members and ensures that they maximize the benefits of membership.
  • Serves as the liaison between civilian and military communities in Mililani, providing vital oversight for Hawaii’s growing defense industry and keeping the business community connected to the state’s second largest industry.
  • Keeps members informed about news and opportunities that impact their businesses while facilitating opportunities for members to promote and build their businesses.
  • Develops events, activities and programs to keep members and the business community abreast of topical issues.
  • Members have the opportunity to attend a wide variety of virtual and in-person seminars, trainings, forums and networking events that support the growth of their companies.
  • Leads engagement between Hawaii’s business community and educational institutions to ensure that Hawaii residents find meaningful careers here at home.
