Relax Here

Most people don't know much about our beautiful town! Preston is one of a kind in so many ways, a gateway to Idaho as we know and love her. We might look like not much at a glance, but once you get in here there is a whole lot to admire and to take advantage of! Want to see a baby cow? Who doesn't! You can definitely find one of those here, especially at this time. I think it's worth remembering that we have plenty of places to take your shoes off and stay a while. The nice thing about a town like this is there is no rush for anything--you can kick back and relax because there's little else to do!

I don't think it's fair to say that doing nothing is a bad thing. The simple life isn't for everyone, and sometimes you want a day that's a little more exciting, but on the day to day or even as a vacation, sometimes having nothing to do is the best thing ever! Too often in our lives, we find ourselves in places where we have to 'go go go' or risk missing out on something important. In our jobs, as parents, as students, we have lists of tasks to complete and there is nothing quite so rewarding as being told to not think about them--and heeding that advice. Here in Preston we do things right, and sometimes doing things right means just pulling up a spot in a park or on your porch and having a lemonade. Don't worry about it! The more often you let your brain decompress like this, the better it will be! So live the Preston way and just let the world pass you by every once in a while.
