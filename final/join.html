Benefits

Joining the Mililani Chamber of Commerce is one big step into easy networking for you and your business! At even our lowest level of membership, you will find yourself with access to a directory of businesses that can help strengethen and partnership with your own! You will also benefit from the overarching interests of the chamber, creating opportunities for you company that you simply cannot attain so easily on your own.

There are a number of different membership options to meet the needs of your business. These each have a membership fee paid annually to the chamber. These fees allow the chamber to assist your business based on your needs. Every year there is a luncheon for participating business members. Though some memberships include tickets to this event, those that do not can purchase the tickets at a later date seperately. We hope to be able to help you get your business to a state of thriving!

Most recently introduced are our sponsorship programs. Find the right sponsor for your business with our Gold package, where we promise we will find you the right partner to spotlight alongside. Or get your name out there with social media and let our team of experts make your mark on the internet. Business changes all the time, so come to one of our seminars to keep up to date on the latest and greatest innovations here in Hawaii.

Membership Levels

  • Non-Profit - $100 annual fee
    Membership in the Chamber of Commerce, directory listing
  • Bronze - $200 annual fee
    All of the above plus yearly business training.
  • Silver - $400 annual fee
    All of the above plus social media posts, luncheon tickets, and sponsor exposure.
  • Gold - $1000 annual fee
    All of the above plus VIP invitation to annual luncheon and valued sponsorships.
