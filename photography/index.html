Your Special Moments

The islands are my home! I grew up in Mililani Town and they say when you are raised in paradise it's hard to want to go anywhere else. So true! I love being here and being able to share this beautiful place with others. Be it for your destination wedding or your military deployment, there are so many memories to make on Oahu.

I know your time here is special, and I know the memories you make with your loved ones are important. You don't want to forget them, and I want help! Got an event here on Oahu? A wedding? A family reunion? Or do you just want a little family photoshoot? I am prepared to help you put these sweet reminders into picture form. I've been working at this since 2012 and I bring a sense of professional fun to the table. I will make sure you get exactly what you want while also making sure we're having a great time. Afraid your shy kiddo won't smile? Trust me, I have a few tricks we can try!

Whether your vision is big or small, I really love meeting new clients and getting to know you. Let me be your next partner in photography, I promise you won't regret it.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your rates?

  • Rates can vary throughout the year due to demand. If you would like a free quote, please get in contact with me and I will be happy to get you an estimate based on your desired dates and event.

How far out in advance do you book?

  • I can book out about a year in advance in most cases, but I am willing to discuss anything within reason. I recommend contacting me as soon as possible with your dates as my schedule can fill up fast, especially during the spring.

Do we have to do our photoshoot on the beach?

  • Of course not! Hawaii is about way more than the water. There are beautiful mountains and forests to consider. The good news about island living is we can get just about anywhere within a small amount of time. So pick your dream spot!

How many people can be in our family photo?

  • While my style works best for a few people, I love big groups! If you have a family reunion and want me to make sure memories are captured, I am happy to work with your group.

I have a wedding on another island. Can you come take photos?

  • Oahu is where I'm based and typically where I do my work. Under special circumstances, however, I might be able to make the journey to a different island. Feel free to contact me and we can discuss whether or not we are a fit.
