Patron Program

Want to join our patron program? We'd love to have you. This program makes sure you get to all the shows you want to get to with first choice of seats. Other perks include backstage access, a sway in our programs, and monthly merchandise. Check out our Patron page for more information!

Group Purchasing

In need of buying tickets in bulk? If your desires exceed our form limits (10 adults, 8 children), please contact us directly by phone or by email. If you would us to contact you, please leave your email and a small note (optional) about your desired group numbers. For certain shows we may be able to offer group pricing.
