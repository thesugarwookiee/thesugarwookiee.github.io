True Spectacle

Built with the grandeur of the days behind us, the Summit Theater has maintained its origins while staying up to date as a place for locals and visitors alike to come and witness some of the greatest stories ever told. With high, arching ceilings and acoustics fine tuned from the beginning, sitting in this theater is like journeying to a different place. Come join us as we light up the night with our golden glow. Be it song, dance, or a dramatic story unfolding before you, we are certain that you will be rivted from piece to piece or act to act.

Join our patron group and get first seat acccess to tickets so that you never miss a show to fall in love with. We believe it will be worth it to come and be swept away by the old classics and contemporary plays, or the swell of the Ridgefield Orchestra. Patrons also get unique access to the backstage during summer events and prime access to our suggestion box: if you want to see it, we want to make it happen.

Our History

Established in 1910, Summit Theater is a historic piece of downtown Cheyenne Point. With deep roots, this original structure has stood the test of time and has seen and heard more stories than most people can fathom in their lifetime. Established by Robert T. Decker alongside his sons, it thrived under their leadership before passing hands to friends, associates, and the like. Under recent new management, the theater's foundation is respected. Even as we upgrade and update this building that we love, we strive to maintain the soul and the intention of the original build. While refurbishment is ongoing, we hope you will join us for discounted tickets and light, local shows brought to you by our talented town.

If you have more questions about our origins, come visit us, or contact us!

How can you get involved?

If you want to be a part of history, be it joining a show or directing your own, contact our talent division for more information. We welcome all experience and ambition levels to come try out our beautiful, historic stage.

Don't be afraid to put yourself out there, we have guidance for anyone green on the scene who want to know what it really means to break a leg, or old pros who simply want a minute of their well-deserved limelight. We have endless stage opportunities, but also behind-the-scenes spaces to fill, so put your best foot forward and show us what you've got!
