Header Font

Work San - A sans-serif font with just enough character in its letters to work well as a header. It is very readable, especially at larger values, but has enough variation to be distinct. On a professional, information site, san-serif is great for the web.

Body Font

Roboto - A sans-serif font that isn't too flashy in any way. Can be read in smaller sizes with ease and pairs well with a slightly bolder header. Clean and professional without feeling too generic. Good for body font as well as navigation points.
