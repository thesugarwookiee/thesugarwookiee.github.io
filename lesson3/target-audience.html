Description

The target audience of this website is primarily people who live in the Preston area and a few select surrounding towns. They are of all ages, but the site is primarily intended for adult use. Information should be accessible to someone with average education. The audience may also include people from out of town who are visiting. As the audience is not overly specific and fairly general, the site should contian basic but solid information.

Personas

Name: Joeseph Werthlin
Gender: Male
Age: 57
Occupation: High school teacher
Education: BA in Education
Family: Married, one grown child, three grandchildren
Needs: Joe is a hard working man. He sometimes checks the weather in the morning on his computer before school starts, but a lot of the time he looks on his ipad because it's more convenient. Sometimes when the kids he teaches get restless at school, he likes to take them outside for their lesson and preferes to know in advance if this is possible before he makes any promises. He also gardens extensively at home and likes to stay up to date on what is going on in town so he can be an involved citizen.
"Is now a good time to be planting my herbs outside?"

Name: Sadie Adler
Gender: Female
Age: 33
Occupation: Mother
Education: BA in Literature
Family: Married, four children, another on the way
Needs: Sadie doesn't live in town, but she visits regularly because she has family in Idaho. She doesn't like to risk bringing her entire family all the way to Preston only to find out the weather means they're going to be cooped up inside all week. She preefers to visit when the weather will be mild, and needs to know as soon in advance as possible when that might be so she can plan ahead. Once the family is town they enjoy going out to fun events and enjoying nicer weather outdoors. As she's a busy mom, she mostly checks the weather on her phone.
"Will it be worth it to go for Christmas this year? Will the weather be mild?"

Scenarios

  • Is it going to be raining at Soda Spring this week?
  • Is the Annual Cornball Hayride still happening this year?
  • There is a major storm coming and and I need to know if I'm safe.
  • I want to go on a picnic, but what day would be good?
  • I'll be in town next month and need to know what to pack for clothing.
